Innovation workshop

2017

Coudre participated in the Innovation Workshop organized by the Human Resources team at Puig, as part of the Packaging Academy project. It was directed by Cristina Noguer.

The collaborative workshop result was a printed perfume diffuser, accompanied by a leather case, a gesmonite base and a glass top.
